Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Code For Us is a workshop series that invites adults in the Twin Cities with little to no tech experience to learn basic web development skills.

Adults aged 35+ are encouraged to participate. BIPOC and LGBTQ+ individuals are particularly encouraged to join.

You can apply for the workshops by filling out the application form available here.

No prior experience is necessary or required to join the workshops. Everyone is welcome!

Technical Questions
